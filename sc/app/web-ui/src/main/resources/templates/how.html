Food recommendation is our game and we are committed to it, by all our heart and soul.


Recommending food on Bananaa is open to all and everyone is invited to do so. However, before we add a restaurant on Bananaa, our team does a decent job of conducting a small research for it.


We go over all the possible social media platforms (you name it), collect recommendations from people, conduct personal interviews and come up with a list of recommended food at a restaurant, before opening it up for you to add more to it.


That way, we do our homework before enlisting a restaurant on Bananaa and make sure you never go wrong with your order.



And what happens after that ?
