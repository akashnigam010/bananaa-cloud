Where are we headed ?

Good food ... something that never makes us full, something that we always want more, but something that is never easy to get.


A lipsmacking and a delicious food is our one true source of happiness, and we believe that everyone must have it.


Which is why we created Bananaa.

What we do ?

Think of it as your personal food assistant that promises to never let you down, when it comes to food.


Bananaa is here to cheer up those unhappy faces.


From helping you decide the best picks at a restaurant, to letting you know which place serves your favourite food around, bananaa takes takes care of it all.

And how ?

Well, it brings together people's choice, what they recommend and love eating around you, does some little tech jugglery and gets it back in the form of food recommendations.


These recommendations are driven by the people themselves. We built it the way it is because we believe in the power of community and the collective wisdom.

Our mission

We have pledged to make this world a better place to eat.


And you have a part to play.


So go on and recommend your favourite food on bananaa and we promise to bring your effort to the best cause.

Making the world, a better place to eat - one step at a time.
